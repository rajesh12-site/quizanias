Game Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2025

1. Play at Your Own Risk

All games on Quizania are skill-based and meant for entertainment and competitive learning. Users are expected to play games at their own discretion and responsibility. If any bug, technical error, server issue, or system failure occurs during gameplay that affects the outcome (win or loss), Quizania will not be held responsible.

2. Bug Exploits and Invalid Wins

If any user wins a large or abnormal amount due to a technical glitch or system error, Quizania reserves the full right to withhold or cancel that reward. In such cases, no explanation is necessary, and affected accounts may be flagged or banned without notice. Attempting to exploit bugs is strictly prohibited.

3. No Liability Clause

Quizania is not responsible for any losses caused due to slow internet, app crash, game timeout, opponent disconnection, or device failure. Users are solely responsible for ensuring a stable internet connection and a functional device before participating in paid games.

🎮 Game Rules & Warnings

Quiz Battle (1v1)

  • Players are matched by category and entry fee.
  • Each player gets 10 questions, same for both players.
  • Correct answers and fastest average response time determine the winner.
  • If a bug delays result submission, the game may be voided.
  • Bot may be assigned if no player is found — bot has 60% win rate.

KBC Mode

  • Fixed entry fee: ₹299. Progressive level-based rewards.
  • Includes 4 lifelines (Skip, 50:50, Audience, Timer Stop).
  • One wrong answer ends the game — no refunds.
  • Gameplay must not be interrupted or results may be lost.

Free Quiz

  • No entry fee required to play.
  • Daily or weekly rewards based on leaderboard rank.
  • Users must answer all questions in time for valid score.
  • Quizania may cancel any free quiz if unfair activity is detected.

Play vs Bot

  • User selects difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard).
  • Bot plays automatically with defined win logic (e.g. 60% win rate).
  • Same 10 questions are given to both.
  • If result cannot be calculated due to bug, no refund is issued.

Entry Fee Quizzes

  • Each entry includes a platform fee (e.g. ₹10 entry = ₹8 in pool).
  • Prize pool is shared based on game rules and winner logic.
  • Quizania reserves the right to hold or cancel results if a bug affects payout.
  • Refunds are not guaranteed after a game starts.

4. Fraud or Abuse Consequences

Any suspicious behavior including match manipulation, bot abuse, answer-sharing, multi-accounting, or exploiting app loopholes may lead to account suspension, ban, forfeiture of winnings, and deletion of user data — without prior warning.

5. Play Responsibly

Quizania encourages responsible gameplay. Users are advised to play for fun and not treat this platform as a full-time income source. Always use funds wisely and avoid overplaying beyond your limits.

6. Final Decision

In any dispute or unresolved scenario, Quizania’s internal records and decision will be considered final and binding. The company reserves the right to change game rules or policies at any time without notice.

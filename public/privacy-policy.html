Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2025

1. Introduction

Quizania ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our platform through the website or app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Details: Name, Email, Mobile Number, State, and City.
  • Authentication Data: Firebase Auth ID, profile photo, and device ID.
  • Wallet & Payment Info: Transaction history, wallet balance, UPI IDs, or bank account info for withdrawals.
  • Usage Data: Game scores, response time, match history, and category preferences.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, OS, and device identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To verify and manage your account.
  • To enable wallet top-ups, winnings, and withdrawals.
  • To match you with appropriate quiz opponents.
  • To detect fraud or misuse of the platform.
  • To personalize game content and user experience.
  • To send important alerts, push notifications, and offers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may share data with:

  • Payment gateways for processing transactions.
  • Firebase (Google) for authentication and cloud functions.
  • Legal or regulatory bodies if required by law.

5. Data Security

We use Firebase Authentication, Firestore, and secure SSL protocols to protect your personal and financial data. All payment data is processed through trusted and encrypted payment partners.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and local storage to remember login status and preferences. These are used to enhance performance and user experience, not for third-party marketing.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion or ban due to violation of terms, your data may be permanently deleted from our servers.

8. Your Rights

  • You can view and update your profile info in the Settings page.
  • You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting support.
  • You can opt-out of promotional emails or push notifications.

9. Children’s Privacy

Quizania is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we become aware, such accounts will be removed.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through in-app alerts or email.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

  • Email: support@quizania.in
